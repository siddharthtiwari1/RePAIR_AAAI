\section{Supplementary Material Organization}
The supplementary material contains four parts. \textbf{Section~A} presents an extended discussion of limitations, covering the scope of the single-sample claim, retain data at inference time, the three-model design, test-time resource constraints, turnaround time, and abuse of the unlearning interface. \textbf{Section~B} reports two additional ablation studies, the choice of the intervention layer (B.1), showing forgetting is complete at every layer with layer~6 giving the best retention, and the rank of STAMP-LR (B.2), showing retention is stable for $r \geq 64$. \textbf{Section~C} traces a complete unlearning request through the pipeline and quantifies robustness to paraphrased, aspect-shifted, and indirect query reformulations. \textbf{Section~D} provides code snippets for STAMP and STAMP-LR.
