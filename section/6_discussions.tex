\section{Limitations and Supplementary Material}
RePAIR is not without limitations: the retain buffer sits uneasily with the GDPR and CCPA motivation, the natural-language unlearning channel is itself an unevaluated attack surface, and end-to-end latency (9.36 and 6.50 minutes) is not yet conversational. The supplementary material discusses these and three further limitations (Section~A), the single-sample comparison under $|\mathcal{D}_f|=1$ in which every training-based baseline fails while STAMP and STAMP-LR reach $Acc_f=0.00$ (Section~B), the full layer, rank, and retain-buffer ablations (Section~C), and a qualitative end-to-end trace (Section~D).
